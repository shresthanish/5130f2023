Abstract: Business Analytics with Website Data

Website data in business analytics can provide valuable insights into customer behavior, preferences, and trends. By analyzing website traffic, conversion rates, and other metrics, businesses can make data-driven decisions to optimize their marketing strategies, improve user experience, and identify potential areas of growth. This paper discusses the ways businesses obtain user consent when collecting website data, common algorithms used to collect website data, and different analytical tools or analyses that can be performed.
